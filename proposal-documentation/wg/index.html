W3C WoT – Working Group

The W3C WoT Working Group (WG) is tasked to write standards-track specifications and test suites. To ensure royalty-free Web standards, participants must be W3C and WoT WG Members and acknowledge the W3C Patent Policy.

Co-Chairs: Michael McCool (Intel) and Sebastian Käbisch (Siemens)

Team contacts: Kazuyuki Ashimura and Dave Raggett
